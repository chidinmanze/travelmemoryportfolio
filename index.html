Travel Memories Portfolio

by Chidinma Nze


Welcome to my portfolio! This site is dedicated to memories of trips I've taken in the last few years. The focus is on scenes and not people, so you'll see buildings, nature, vehicles and streets as the main focus of most of the images here. The majority of pictures were taken with an Android phone of some type, though a few might have been taken on a digital camera. I've also added in a few pictures from Michigan, where I live.
